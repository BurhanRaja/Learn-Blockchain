Transactions - fields

tx = transaction

1. Nonce: tx count for the account
2. Gas Price: price per unit of gas (in wei)
3. Gas Limit: Max. limit the tx can use
4. Value: amount in wei sent
5. To: address of whom you are sending it to
6. Data: data that is sent with the tx
7. v,r,s: components of tx signature

Every Transaction:

Gas Price = Gas limit/Usage * Gas Fees
